Featured Speakers

Discover the brilliant minds leading Africa's digital revolution

Tsepiso Mokobori

Tsepiso Mokobori works at a natively onchain gaming tech company and contributes to a public goods initiative led by young African developers building tools to tackle the continent's biggest challenges through emerging internet technologies. She advocates for open-source, decentralised tech, inclusive education, and communities empowered by honest, evolving technology.

Mr. Sam Mohatle

Head of digital Banking, FNB Lesotho

Mr. Sam Mohatle is Head of Digital Banking at FNB Lesotho with 15+ years' experience in fintech, IT, and banking. He leads the bank's digitisation efforts and champions financial inclusion through innovative models like CashPlus. Sam is passionate about SMME empowerment and regularly speaks at international fintech and banking forums.

Mr. Phello Ralibakha

ITSM Manager LNIG Hollard

Phello is a digital transformation leader with over six years' experience, currently serving as ITSM Manager at LNIG Hollard. With a Computer Science degree and MBA studies underway, he drives innovation at the intersection of tech and business, focusing on AI in financial services. A strong advocate for "Trust and Inspire" leadership, he champions inclusive, people-first approaches to digital change.

Mr. Joshua Phoho Setipa

Former Managing Director, UN Technology Bank for Least Developed Countries

Mr. Setipa is a development and innovation leader with over 20 years' experience in trade, economic policy, and international partnerships. As the first Managing Director of the UN Technology Bank, he launched innovation labs and built global partnerships, including with NASA's Jet Propulsion Lab. He previously served as Lesotho's Minister of Trade and Industry, CEO of the Lesotho National Development Corporation, and Senior Advisor to the WTO Director-General. He also held leadership roles at the World Bank.

Zanele Selebalo

Zanele Selebalo is a dynamic Mosotho digital innovator, cybersecurity analyst, and web/app developer, passionately driving Africa's tech evolution. Currently serving as Chief Services Officer at Selemela Learning Network, she leads strategic digital transformation initiatives that empower young people through education, innovation, and inclusion.

Lerato Molisana

Lerato Molisana is a Maths confidence coach, educator and writer who is refinding how we teach, learn and relate to Mathematics. With a bacground in Mathemstical Statistics and a MFA in Creative Writing, She brings a rare fusion of analytical thinking and narattive intelligence to her work.

H.E Harry MacDonald

British High Commissioner

"Digital Diplomacy in the Age of Disinformation: Upholding Ethics, Trust, and Global Coperation"
