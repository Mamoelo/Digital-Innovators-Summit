Privacy Policy

How we protect and use your information

Digital Innovators Summit Privacy Policy

Data Collection

We collect information when you register for the summit, including:

  • Name and contact details
  • Institutional affiliation
  • Session preferences
  • Payment information (if applicable)
  • Dietary requirements (for in-person events)

Data Usage

Your information is used to:

  • Process your registration and manage event access
  • Communicate important summit details and updates
  • Personalize your event experience
  • Improve future events through analytics
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Protection

We implement security measures to protect your personal information in accordance with best practices and Lesotho's data protection regulations. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Secure data storage solutions

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Enhance user experience
  • Analyze site usage
  • Deliver targeted content

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Third-Party Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Event partners and sponsors (with your consent)
  • Service providers who assist with event operations
  • Legal authorities when required by law

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. The latest version will always be posted on our website with an updated revision date.

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at:
